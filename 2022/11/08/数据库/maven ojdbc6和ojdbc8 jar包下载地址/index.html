maven ojdbc6和ojdbc8 jar包下载地址

查找maven依赖网址：

https://mvnrepository.com/

jar文件仓库地址：

https://repo1.maven.org/maven2/

ojdbc6.jar：

https://repo1.maven.org/maven2/com/oracle/database/jdbc/ojdbc6/11.2.0.4/ojdbc6-11.2.0.4.jar

ojdbc8.jar：

https://repo1.maven.org/maven2/com/oracle/database/jdbc/ojdbc8/
https://repo1.maven.org/maven2/com/oracle/ojdbc/ojdbc8/

Oracle官网下载地址(只找到ojdbc8和ojdbc14)：

https://www.oracle.com/database/technologies/appdev/jdbc-downloads.html
